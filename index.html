Rotherham Housing Developer Summit 2019 — Booking ends 20 November

Gathering together developers, housing associations, funders and advisers from across the region, this Rotherham Council event is focused on making connections, tackling the local housing industry challenges and doing business.

Taking place on 5th December at New York Stadium in Rotherham, the summit will showcase the innovative work we are doing in Rotherham and new development opportunities being brought to the market.

Chairing the summit will be Jayne Dowle. Jayne lives in Barnsley and writes about property, homes and architecture for a number of national publications including The Times, The Sunday Times, Grand Designs and House Beautiful. She also writes a twice-weekly social affairs column for The Yorkshire Post and is a regional judge for the Federation of Master Builders' Awards.

Conference

The first part of the Summit starts with a welcome address from our Chair Jayne Dowle who writes about property, homes and architecture for a number of national publications.

There will then be a number of informative speaker sessions from some of the Council’s senior leaders. Finishing with a Q&A session where you will get to ask questions to the key decision makers from the Council about the issues that matter to you the most.

12pm – 1.30pm (lunch served at 12.45pm)

Marketplace and Exhibition

In the second part of the Summit you will have time to network and access our marketplace and exhibition area where you will get to speak to various Council departments and partners as well as see upcoming opportunities.

There will also be a dedicated meeting place for you to make those connections and do business.
1.30pm Event closes

Marketplace and Exhibition

We are keen to continue to progress our housing growth plans and this year's event will focus on Accelerating Delivery.

As an attendee, you will have exclusive access to our marketplace and exhibition area where you will get speak to other Council departments such as Planning and Affordable Housing for support and help as well as have early access to opportunities. There will also be a dedicated meeting place for you to make those connections and do business.

Last year's event

Last year's Rotherham Housing Developer Summit held in November 2018 was another great success. The event gave us the opportunity to engage with many of you directly, we were able to discuss future opportunities to work together and make many new business connections.

There was a real buzz in the room as delegates found out about our aspirations for the Town Centre and Bassingthorpe Farm. as well as hearing about the some of the site opportunities in the newly adopted Local Plan and opportunities for new housing across some of our Council owned sites.

We thank everyone who attended for your generous comments and how we can continue to improve.
