What is the Rotherham Housing Developer Summit?

Join us on Thursday 5th December for our fourth annual Housing Developer Summit. We are keen to continue to progress our housing growth plans, and with this in mind, this year's event will focus on Accelerating Delivery.

The Rotherham Housing Developer Summit brings together people from across the region to deliver housing growth by tackling the local housing industry challenges we face and to make the most of local opportunities.

This year's event will showcase the innovative work we are doing in Rotherham and new development opportunities being brought to the market.

Marketplace and Exhibition

We are keen to continue to progress our housing growth plans and this year's event will focus on Accelerating Delivery.

As an attendee, you will have exclusive access to our marketplace and exhibition area where you will get speak to other Council departments such as Planning and Affordable Housing for support and help as well as have early access to opportunities. There will also be a dedicated meeting place for you to make those connections and do business.

Excellent Networking Opportunities

The Rotherham Housing Developer Summit facilitates networking opportunities by bringing together people from across the housing industry. This year you will get to hear from speakers across the housing industry and directly speak to key decision makes about the issues that matter to you.

We encourage partnership working, learning from each other and innovation.

Rotherham Housing Developer Summit 2018

Last year's Rotherham Housing Developer Summit in November 2018 was another great success. The event gave us the opportunity to engage with many of you directly, we were able to discuss future opportunities to work together and make many new business connections.

There was a really buzz in the room as delegates found out about our aspirations for the Town Centre and Bassingthorpe Farm. As well as hearing about the some of the site opportunities in the newly adopted Local Plan and opportunities for new housing across some of our Council owned sites.

We thank everyone who attended for your generous comments and how we can continue to improve.
